Who are
we?

Tshomo Studios is a video game and animation production company in Johannesburg, South Africa. We specialise in capturing untold cultures in interactive
game and animation compositions. Our young-hearted staff is eclectic and fun, and we have the urge to unravel stories of heritage and imagination.

Mission Statment

We cultivate an interest in finding stories of untold cultures and find fascinating and interactive ways to tell them.

Vision Statment

To be part of a child’s upbringing.
We're on a mission to revolutionise the stories that shape the next generation.

Lack of Diversity
in Content

The issue of limited representation or diversity in game narratives and characters persists as a significant concern within the gaming industry. Despite strides towards inclusivity, many games continue to fall short in depicting a wide range of identities, backgrounds, and experiences. This lack of representation not only undermines the potential for richer storytelling but also alienates diverse audiences who seek meaningful connections with characters that reflect their own lived realities.

embracing
inclusivity

Developing games that incorporate diverse stories, characters, and perspectives is essential for appealing to a broader audience. By embracing inclusivity, our game developers can create experiences that resonate with players from various backgrounds and identities, fostering a sense of belonging and representation within gaming communities. This commitment to diversity not only enriches the storytelling potential of games but also cultivates a more inclusive and welcoming environment for all players to engage with and enjoy.

"Games with characters, stories, and settings reflecting African experiences empower players, fostering cultural pride and identity."
